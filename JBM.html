About

Born and bred in Northumberland, in the middle of nowhere Jonny had the perfect setting to become a musician – an encouraging family, not many friends – the opportunity was begging. Coming from a stage school background he always wanted to progress to the singer/songwriter position, trying to bring together influences of modern artists such as Ben Howard and Ed Sheeran, with the deeply traditional folk roots of the border county he has always lived in. He, says ‘ I am not entirely sure which genre I fit into, I guess I have never really been one to fit in – as long as people still enjoy it I will just keep writing the way I do!’
